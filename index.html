Explore Clubs

The Science and Technology Council (SnTC) at IIT Mandi serves as the central hub for all student-led technical innovation, learning, and exploration on campus. We bring together a diverse set of clubs, projects, and events that fuel curiosity, foster collaboration, and empower students to build and showcase their skills beyond the classroom. From robotics and AI to electronics, coding, and design, SnTC provides the platform, resources, and mentorship to turn ideas into impact. Whether you're a beginner eager to learn or a maker ready to build, there's a place for you here.
